Cell Organelles

Nucleus

Controls all activities of the cell and stores DNA.

Golgi Apparatus

Packages and transports proteins and lipids.

Endoplasmic Reticulum

Helps in transport and synthesis inside the cell.

Ribosomes

Protein factories of the cell.

Plastids

Help in photosynthesis in plant cells.

Lysosomes

Digest waste materials inside the cell.

Mitochondria

Produce energy for the cell.
